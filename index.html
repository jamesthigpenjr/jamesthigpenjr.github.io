ABOUT

I'm a Flint, Michigan raised artist, living and working in New York. It was second grade when I dressed up for my schools career day as an artist, and believe it or not, I’ve practiced becoming my definition of an artist every since. During my years at the University of Michigan-Flint I grew an interest in fine art and an increased frustration with those around me equating graphic design to advertisement. I seen myself as more than a salesman, but it wasn’t until my move to New York in 2013 that I began to understand how I would blur the line between the two worlds.

My work is inspired by an array of things; childhood, unanswered questions, conversations, pop culture, etc. I enjoy allowing the audience to determine their own meaning to each piece. This creates deep conversation, and I like to think it creates a ripple that will allow others to create as well.

-James Thigpen Jr.>
